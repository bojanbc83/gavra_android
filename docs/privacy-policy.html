Introduction

Gavra 013 ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Gavra 013 mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect precise GPS location data to provide transportation services, including pickup and drop-off locations.
  • Contact Information: Phone number for communication between drivers and passengers.
  • Account Information: Name and basic profile information for account creation.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our transportation service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage with appropriate security measures in place.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information may be shared only:

  • Between passengers and drivers for ride coordination

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
